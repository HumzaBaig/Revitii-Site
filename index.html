A Walkthrough of Our Process

First Meeting

Company A plans on passing this company to the next generation, and needs to 'future-proof' their business processes. Currently, they input the same data to multiple locations including Quickbooks, Clover, and their own personal spreadsheets. Another issue is that many managers send important info to the admin office on paper/calls.

Business Strategy

We take the info given to us and come to the conclusion that company A needs a customized and centralized web-application that has all the forms necessary for the managers along with data analytics and requirements for managers.

Proposal

The proposal is split into plans. The features offered in the proposal include a web application with user-authentication, multiple forms such as finances, employee salaries, etc., back-end connections to Quickbooks and Clover for automation and a data analytics dashboard using machine-learning algorithms.

Select Plan

Company A chooses to go with all the features mentioned in the proposal, but would like to include a data analytics dashboard without machine-learning. Instead, Company A knows exactly what data points they would like to be included.

Agreement

After having chosen the right plan for them, we send a contract with deliverables and deadlines for the project for Company A to e-sign before we begin our work.

Lets Work!

Company A sits back as our team begins working on the project. As the milestones established in the contract are met, we communicate and deploy the project. We also keep up good communication and transparency with Company A to make sure they're satisfied with the entire process.
